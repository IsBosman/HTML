Bio

Isaiah Boswell

Beginner 3D artist and Software Engineer

Brigham Young University-Idaho

Hi, I'm Isaiah! I started learning 3d art about a month and a half ago, with my first project being a donut animation! I'm twenty two years old and the oldest of six children. I can't really claim to be from any specific place as I've lived in Mississippi, Utah, Kansas, Ohio, Colorado, England, and now Idaho. I enjoy sci-fi and fantasy, David Weber, Robin Hobb, Brandon Sanderson, Issac Asimov, which is where much of my work will be pointed in the future!
