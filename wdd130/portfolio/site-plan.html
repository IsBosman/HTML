Content

Home page

I'm pretty new to the world of 3d art and animation. That being said I haven't exactly found a style for myself. My focus is to branch out, become proficient and to find a style that I enjoy and that you will enjoy. As of right now, I've been leaning towards photorealism with the watch, the Isometric room, and my Godrays project.

I'm not looking to do comissions right now, my repertoire is not yet advanced enough for me to feel confident seeking comissions. Though if you enjoy my work so far and want a comission done I'm 100% willing to work with you and create what you have in mind.

Contact Me
If you are interested in comissions, you can email me, or you can reach me through Facebook or Instagram.

Bio

Hi, I'm Isaiah! I started learning 3d art about a month and a half ago, with my first project being a donut animation! I'm twenty two years old and the oldest of six children. I can't really claim to be from any specific place as I've lived in Mississippi, Utah, Kansas, Ohio, Colorado, England, and now Idaho. I enjoy sci-fi and fantasy, David Weber, Robin Hobb, Brandon Sanderson, Issac Asimov, which is where much of my work will be pointed in the future!
