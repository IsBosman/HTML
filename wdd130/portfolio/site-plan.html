Content

Home page

Ease
I plan on people coming to my site because I want to show them my work without having to scroll through files and images on my phone or computer.

Style, Fit, and Preference
What I'd like someone to learn when they go onto the site is what I'm capable of, what my style is, and if I'm the right fit for what they want done. I also want people to see where I started and what I've learned. I'd like to have a page dedicated to me revisiting my old work and making it better based on what I've learned. But the primary purpose for this is to let people see my work, and decide if they want a commission, if they simply want to view it, or if I'm not the right fit for them.

Commission Potential
Eventually I'd like to do comissions, it's not something that I'm ready for right now, but it's something that I look forward to doing in the future, one day I want to create something that makes waves in the community so I have a lot of time and a lot of energy that I still need to put into this.

Contact Me
My website will be the only place that they'll be able to contact me if they want comissions done. It's also the only place they'll be able to see a full collection of my projects in one place, rather than scattered about in files or online.
