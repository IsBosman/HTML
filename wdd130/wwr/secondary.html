Colorado River

High in the pristine Rocky Mountain wilderness of Colorado, the mighty Colorado River begins. After winding its way across Colorado, the river enters some of the most spectacular river rafting destinations in the world. These unparalleled include famous Westwater Canyon, Southwest Sampler, and Cataract Canyon through Canyonlands National Park.

Animas River

Experience the excitement of a Durango rafting adventure! Splash through “Smelter,” “Santa Rita,” “Sawmill,” and “Pinball” rapids as part of your excursion down the Animas River! Your guide will share the history of the region and stories on local traditions. Families, reunions, groups, first-timers and rafting veterans alike find this popular and convenient guided rafting trip to be the highlight of their vacation!

San Juan River

The San Juan River offers world class Utah-Colorado river rafting and the adventure of a lifetime for all ages through one of the world’s greatest outdoor museums. We offer daily 8-hour trips up to 10-day expeditions. The longer the trip the more opportunity there is for hiking to ancient Puebloan sites to view rock art and cliff dwellings much like you’d see in Mesa Verde National Park. The San Juan river is divided into two different sections known as the Upper and Lower Canyon. The Upper Canyon trip starts in Bluff and floats 27 miles to Mexican Hat. The Lower canyon begins at Mexican Hat and floats 58 miles to Clay Hills crossing near the northern end of Lake Powell. We run river trips March – October.

General Info

We also run routes along different rivers that don't require a booking! Just show up and you're ready to go, these routes offer shorter, exciting excursions that take anywhere between 45 minutes to 5 hours! These additional rivers include the Arkansas River, the Green River, the Blue River, Dolores River, Gunnison River, and Yampa River.

On the difficulty scale of 1-10, with 10 being the most difficult, the lowest is Gunnison river at an 8.

There are a few locations along the Animas and San Juan rivers that offer 3 hour relaxing trips as well. Those each rank at a 2 and a sightseeing guide will take you on a wildlife tour!
